Subscription Plan

Upon purchase, you are entitled to utilise the purchased services within one (1) year from your subscription activation date.

Your subscription shall automatically renew for a further period of one (1) year upon the expiry of the current year. You may cancel the automatic renewal anytime through the settings in your account. Upon cancelling automatic renewal, your subscription shall continue and terminate at the end of the current year of subscription. For clarity purposes, your designated payment method shall be charged three (3) days prior to the actual date of renewal.

Should you terminate your account with us during the subscription, you forfeit all unused allocation upon termination.

Should you request a cancellation and refund of the current subscription:

  • Within five (5) business days of such purchase or automatically renewed purchase and you have not utilized the subscription plan and any free credits as given (if any), you shall be entitled to a full refund of the purchase price;
  • After five (5) business days of your purchase or automatically renewed purchase and you have not utilized the subscription plan and any free credits as given (if any), you shall be charged a cancellation fee amounting to fifty percent (50%) of your purchase price; or
  • If you have utilized the subscription plan and any free credits as given (if any), a price adjustment will be charged based on your utilization.


Vectr reserves the right to determine such price adjustment and refund at its sole discretion.

For more information, please contact us at info@vectr.com.

Credits

Upon purchase and/or activation, you are entitled to utilise the purchased credits. The purchased credits do not expire and can be accumulated over time.

Within five (5) business days of such purchase and you have not utilised any of the purchased credits, you shall be entitled to a full refund of the purchase price.
